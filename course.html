Our Courses

Computer Science - Duration: 4 years

Learn programming languages, algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and computer systems. Covers topics like AI, machine learning, and cybersecurity.

Information Technology - Duration: 4 years

Focus on IT infrastructure, networking, databases, web development, and system administration. Includes project management and IT security.

Computer Science with Data Science - Duration: 4 years

Combines computer science fundamentals with data analysis, statistics, machine learning, and big data technologies.

Computer Science with IOT and Cybersecurity - Duration: 4 years

Integrates IoT technologies, embedded systems, and cybersecurity practices with core computer science principles.

Electronics - Duration: 4 years

Study electronic circuits, digital systems, microprocessors, and signal processing. Includes lab work on hardware design.

Electrical - Duration: 4 years

Cover electrical engineering fundamentals: power systems, control systems, electromagnetism, and electrical machines.

Electronics and Telecommunication - Duration: 4 years

Explore communication systems, wireless technologies, optical fibers, and electronic design for telecom applications.

Electronics and computer science - Duration: 4 years

Blend electronics with software development, focusing on embedded systems, robotics, and computer hardware interfaces.

Electrical and electronics - Duration: 4 years

Comprehensive study of electrical and electronic systems, including automation, instrumentation, and power electronics.

Mechanical - Duration: 4 years

Learn mechanics, thermodynamics, materials science, and design. Includes CAD, manufacturing processes, and engineering analysis.

Mechanical and Robotics - Duration: 4 years

Focus on mechanical engineering with robotics, automation, control systems, and mechatronics.

Civil - Duration: 4 years

Cover structural engineering, construction, transportation, environmental engineering, and urban planning.

Biotechnology - Duration: 4 years

Study biological sciences applied to technology: genetic engineering, bioinformatics, pharmaceuticals, and bioethics.
